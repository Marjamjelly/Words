# Words
A collection of words. Most likely about geography. Who knows.  

This is also for my typing practice. Most words will be lowercase, and countries and other regions may be shortened in a way acceptable by the quiz site [JetPunk](https://www.jetpunk.com/). It also means that names of things that are 2 words, (e.g. New York) will be condensed into one word. (e.g. newyork)
